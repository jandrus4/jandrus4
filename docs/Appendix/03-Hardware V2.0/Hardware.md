title: Hardware V2.0
---

If I were to redo this design, I would start with understanding how footprint sizes are written. Several of my chosen footprints were smaller than I had intended, resulting in significant difficulty with soldering. I would also spend more time discussing my design with the teaching team, to ensure my design actually works. I would spend more time reviewing each decision I make about the hardware, to verify everything fits within the parameters that have been set before me and I will not have any more significant difficulty with my chosen parts. I would also through-hole jumpers to make rewiring my PCB easier, because trying to rewire the PCB with surface-mount jumpers was extremely difficult.
